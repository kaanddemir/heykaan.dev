How It Works.

Understand any web page in a simple and clear way. Everything runs directly in your browser.

1

Content Extraction

Extract clean, readable content from any web page. We remove navigation, ads, and noise to isolate the main text for accurate readability analysis.

2

Readability Analysis

Analyze text complexity with clear, explainable metrics. We calculate sentence length, word density, and readability scores locally in your browser.

3

AI Summary

Generate fast, on-page summaries with built-in AI. When available, Chrome's native models create concise summaries directly in your browser.

4

Reader Tools

Rebuild the page for focused, distraction-free reading. Use Focus Mode and reading aids to reduce cognitive load and stay on track.
