Why Privacy Matters

While browsing online, it is common to find useful information mixed with personal details, distracting elements, and sections that were never meant to be shared publicly. Instead of editing screenshots or copying text into other tools, Soft Blackout allows certain parts of a page to be hidden while the rest stays readable and intact. This simple control helps people share content safely, stay focused, and reduce visual noise without changing the original page structure. Over time, using Soft Blackout makes browsing feel calmer, more private, and fully under control.

Overview

Soft Blackout is a lightweight, privacy-first Chrome Extension designed to help you hide sensitive content or remove distractions. Control your content, focus on reading without clutter, or unleash your creativity with blackout poetry.
